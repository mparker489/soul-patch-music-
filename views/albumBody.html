Romantic Works by Keaton Henson

Romantic Works is the 3rd album by British musician Keaton Henson. This album was written to be completely instrumental and features cellist Ren Ford. It was released in 2014 and features tracks such as "Earnestly Yours" and "Healah Dancing"
